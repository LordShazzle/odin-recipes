Garlic Chicken

Are you ready for the best chicken dish ever?

Yes! This is one of the best Garlic Chicken you can try for yourself, its always delicious!

Ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup seasoned bread crumbs
  • ½ cup milk
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - pounded thin
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder, or to taste
  • 1 cup oil for frying, or as needed
